I'm Cory Francis, an engineering leader and independent consultant in Seattle. I spent nearly a decade building and running infrastructure, cloud operations, and SRE teams, most recently as Director of Developer Operations at A Place for Mom, where I led a global team of eight SREs across three countries and served as the primary technology advisor to senior leadership.

Some of the work I'm proud of from that time:

- Drove a legacy monolith into more than 100 containerized microservices on AWS
- Scaled production deployments from once every two weeks to more than 100 per week
- Owned a $250K per month cloud budget and cut $110K per year in redundant SaaS spend
- Unified CI/CD from Jenkins, Ansible, and TeamCity onto a single GitHub Actions platform

Before that I managed Windows infrastructure at Expeditors International, thousands of servers across 350+ offices in 100+ countries, where I was the resident PowerShell subject matter expert.

## What I'm doing now

Leadership pulled me away from the keyboard for a long time. Consulting is how I'm getting back in touch with my roots: building open source tools, running cloud migrations, and doing hands-on work for Pacific Northwest organizations that need things to actually work rather than just look good in a slide deck.

I'm open to engineering leadership and senior platform roles. If you're hiring, or you have a problem worth solving, get in touch.
